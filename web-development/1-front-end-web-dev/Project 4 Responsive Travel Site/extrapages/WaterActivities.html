Diving into the Pacific

Diving into the Pacific Ocean is a sight to behold. From the coral reefs to the variety of fish a trip into the ocean here will be like no other place in the world. You will find something new. The depths of the ocean facinating. Here at Fiji, we pride ourselves in the beauty of the ocean

Snorkelling on the Coast

Need something to do less deep or something more family-friendly to see under the water-- Snorkelling is a great choice to experience what lies just beneath the surface of the ocean. With the backdrop of the island and the light blue ocean, and the unparalleled habitats of marine creatures, think of what will you find here!

Swimming Fun

Snokelling too scary-- Well, for the more experienced, the ocean is still ripe for swimming in. Otherwise the resorts here will provide pools to swim in.

Boating on the Water

How did we first get to the islands-- It was by boats or some floating craft. We can take a step further in leisure by boating around the many islands in our nation. Sandy beaches, mysterious jungles, and the great wide Blue is an expericence waiting to happen to you.
