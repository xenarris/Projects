About

PineAppWhack Company

The PineAppWhack Company serves to provide the best in quality pineapple food products. Starting from Year One, there has been zero complaints, problems, and questions. Each pineapple by us is grown locally at the far reaches of North Jupiter and delivered to you under the most harrowing of circumstances to prove to you how important it is to us to provide pineapples you need. Better yet, everything we sell costs only what customers are willing to pay! Contact us below for orders and details.

Why Pineapples?

We tried to market the Pinecone as the PineConeS Co. to the masses, but sadly wasn't well recieved. Something about its abundance or being inedible. Then we tried pineapples, and that took off! To our founder, it was out of this world. We then changed our company name to PineAppWhack Co. to better market our already great products.
